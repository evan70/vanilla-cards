Getting Started with Vanilla JS

5 min read March 15, 2024 John Doe
Article Resources

Vanilla JavaScript is the foundation of the modern web. In this article, we'll explore why choosing plain JS over heavy frameworks can lead to better performance and more maintainable code.

Why Vanilla?

Frameworks come and go, but the web standards remain. By learning and using Vanilla JS, you're investing in skills that will never become obsolete.

Performance

Every byte counts. When you skip the framework, you're skipping the overhead of downloading, parsing, and executing thousands of lines of code before your app even starts.

Simplicity

Modern browser APIs are incredibly powerful. Things that used to require jQuery or complex framework logic can now often be done in a single line of standard JS.

"Simplicity is the soul of efficiency."

Conclusion

Start small, stay close to the platform, and only add abstractions when you truly need them. Your users (and your lighthouse score) will thank you.
